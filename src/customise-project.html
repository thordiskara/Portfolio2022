Customise Me

ask

The task was to redesign Customise Me's current website to make it more engaging and to optimize their user flow. They wanted a new solution for their customisation page to make it more understandable and fun for the customer.

solution

As a group we created a new way for customers to customise their soaps by having a step by step form that the customer could fill out, that was more visual and understandible for the user.


My focus area was to design the UI, create a database and develop the web solution.
