Silfen


A Danish accessory and clothing brand that mainly focuses on designing handbags.


Silfen's sustainability page

client

Silfen

role

  • User and market research
  • Visual Storytelling
  • Redesign

ask

To optimize their sustainability page in a more engaging, dynamic and trustworthy way. One of the brand's core values is to have a strong focus on sustainability and try to be as environmentally conscious as possible. Therefor they asked for CSR content for their social media to introduce customers to their company values.

solution

As a group we decided the best way for Silfen to gain customers trust was transparancy. Therefor we created a time line to highlight what the company had done over the years to be more sustainable. We also created video content for their social media channels that emphasized their strive for sustainable production


My focus areas were market and user research to see what customers looked for when shoping with sustainable brands, prototyping and testing of the sustainability page and production of their SoMe content.
